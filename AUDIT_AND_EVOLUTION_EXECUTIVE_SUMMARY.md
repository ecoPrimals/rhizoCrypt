## 🚀 The Infant Discovery Model

### What It Means

Traditional approach (hardcoded):
```
Primal knows: "BearDog is at 127.0.0.1:9500"
              "LoamSpine is at 127.0.0.1:9600"
              "NestGate is at 127.0.0.1:9700"
Result: Locked to specific vendors
```

**rhizoCrypt's infant discovery:**
```
rhizoCrypt at birth knows:
  ├── Self: "I am RhizoCrypt"
  ├── Universal Adapter: "Songbird is at X"
  └── Everything else: Discovered at runtime

rhizoCrypt after bootstrap:
  ├── Queries: "Who provides signing?"
  ├── Receives: ANY service with signing capability
  ├── Operates: With whatever is discovered
  └── Result: Works with ANY provider
```
